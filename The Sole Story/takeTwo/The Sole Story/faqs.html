Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What type of material is used?

A: All designs can be created on nearly any canvas material with the use of acrylic paints.

Q: What protects the design?

A: Each piece is coated with a clear sealer that protects against weather conditions and the sun.

Q: Are the items waterproof?

A: Items are weather resistant, but should not be washed or worn in harsh weather conditions.

Q: How much do you charge?

A: Items very depending on how detailed they are, but it is $15 per hour of painting.

Q: What is the average paint time?

A: 6-8 hours.

Q: How long until my order is complete?

A: Depending on how many orders I have, it can take anywhere from a few days to 8 weeks. I suggest submitting an inquiry if you are concerned with a deadline.

Q: What is the ordering process?

A: You can submit an inquiry via my contact page or head over to www.etsy.com/thesolestory, select custom order, and write a small description of what you want on the item.

Q: Can I provide a used item?

A: Yes, as long as the item is freshly laundered and not excessively used or worn.

Q: Can an item be replicated?

A: The mission of The Sole Story is to tell one-of-a-kind stories that represent each individual. While the same theme can be used, no two items will ever be the same to ensure uniqueness.
